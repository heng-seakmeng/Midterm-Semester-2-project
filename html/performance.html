Under the Skin

Technology

01

Carbon Fibre Monocoque

The MonoCell II-T chassis is formed from a single carbon fibre structure — weighing just 75kg yet strong enough to protect at race speeds. It forms the structural backbone of every McLaren road car.

02

Twin-Turbo V8 Engine

The 4.0L M840T engine produces 710HP and 770Nm of torque. A flat-plane crankshaft gives it a high-revving character normally reserved for naturally aspirated engines.

03

Proactive Chassis Control II

Interconnected hydraulic suspension replaces traditional anti-roll bars, allowing the car to react to road conditions in milliseconds while maintaining composure at any speed.

04

Active Aerodynamics

The rear wing deploys automatically at high speed and under braking, generating up to 185kg of downforce without driver input — keeping you planted at every corner.

Form Follows Function

Aerodynamics

Every surface on a McLaren serves an aerodynamic purpose. Air is guided, manipulated, and accelerated to generate downforce, reduce drag, and manage heat — all simultaneously.
